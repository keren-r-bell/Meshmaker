# On the Subject of the Color Palette

The color palette is designed to make quick alterations to the colors after they've been created between the mesh's existing points.

In the center, you can see the current color.  
On the left and right edges, you can change the color's **Hue**.  
The two stacked swatches on the left increase / decrease the color's **Saturation**.
The two stacked swatches on the right increase / decreate the color's **Brightness**.
